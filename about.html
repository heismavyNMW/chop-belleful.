Our Story

Born in a Kitchen, Built on Love

The story of Chop Belleful is the story of a family that believed good food should be accessible to everyone — and took action to make it happen.

How It Started

A Grandmother's Recipe, A Daughter's Dream

Chop Belleful started in 2019 in a small family kitchen in Calabar. Our founder Mama Nkoyo had watched her mother cook for the community for decades — generous, fragrant pots of soup that brought the whole neighbourhood together.

When Mama Nkoyo's daughter Esther saw an opportunity to share that food beyond the compound walls, she made a decision: package it, brand it, and take it to the streets. Not as a restaurant — as a promise. A promise that every Nigerian deserves a belly-filling, soul-warming meal at a fair price.

That promise became Chop Belleful. Five years later, we've served over 500 customers, built a loyal community, and never once changed the recipe.

What We Stand For

Our Values

These aren't just words on a wall. They're the principles that guide every pot we stir and every order we deliver.

🌿

Freshness First

We buy ingredients daily. Nothing from yesterday makes it into your plate. Fresh markets, fresh produce, fresh flavour — every single day.

🤝

Community Roots

We source locally, employ locally, and serve locally. Chop Belleful is a Calabar business that gives back to the community that made it.

⚖️

Fair Pricing

Good food should not be a luxury. We price our meals so that working families, students, and everyday Nigerians can eat well without breaking the bank.

🧼

Clean Kitchen Standards

You can't see our kitchen but we cook as if you can. Hygiene, cleanliness, and food safety are non-negotiable standards in every meal we make.
