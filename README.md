## Our Story: A Daughter’s Dream
Chop Belleful started in 2019 in a small family kitchen in Calabar. Our founder, Mama Nkoyo, had watched her mother cook for the community for decades — generous, fragrant pots of soup that brought the whole neighborhood together.

When Mama Nkoyo’s daughter, Esther, saw an opportunity to share that food beyond the compound walls, she made a decision: package it, brand it, and take it to the streets. Not as a restaurant — but as a promise.

Five years later, we’ve served over 500 customers, built a loyal community, and never once changed the recipe.
